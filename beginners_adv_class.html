Beginner to Intermediate Website Hacking course.

A beginner to Intermediate ethical hacking course with website attack & remediation in focus. Unlike other ethical hacking courses online, this one focuses on website hacking and techniques involved. The methods here are are still very much applicable to real word penetration tests. The course makes sure beginners understand and is able to learn how website hacking works with easy to follow lessons. Intermediates too are not left out as other techniques and programing is added. So the uniqueness of this course considers beginners with no programing skills and amateurs or intermediates with more advance techniques to up-skill their skill sets.

+ What you will learn from this course as a beginner.

As beginner using this course, you will learn the following with little or no programing skills;

- Essentials of what it takes to be ethical hacker.

- Preparing to attack a website

- Methods of gathering information about the target.

- 3 ways of Gaining access to targets website & exploiting it.

- How to better secure a website.

+ What intermediate will learn from this course.

If you have some programing knowledge or have some experience in the field, then you are in this category, and as such you we will add some muscles to your weight, hence you will be learning;

- How to script your own website information gathering tool.

- How to create & upload your own backdoor to a target website.

- Exploiting advance web-hacking technique for finding vulnerabilities quickly. + The normal web-hacking techniques for beginners.

- Advanced form of preventing hacks and detecting them plus industry best practices.

+ Requirements.

- Beginners: Either Mobile device (smartphone) or PC will be enough for learning + enough data.

- Intermediates: Either Mobile device (smartphone) & PC are needed + enough data obviously.

Why this course will meet your needs,

after you must have mastered the beginners aspect of things, forging ahead to grasp better and strong knowledge is needed hence, the intermediate website hacking session of this course will meet your need, giving value for your money.
